Sculpture/Drawing


This was a final project of my sculpture class. It took me a three-class session to complete it.

I choose God Ganesh as my final project because I always worship and admire God Ganesh. Also, God Ganesh is one of the main Gods in Hindu culture. From the start of my sculpture class, I knew I wanted to make a statue of God Ganesh whose upper body is supported by his hand. But I was unsure of how I would proceed it. Even though the structure was not fully supportive, I wanted to create it as exactly how I had imagined. After firing the sculpture in the Kiln, I was very happy yet surprised to see my sculpture not being broken. This project taught me a lesson that, "If you believe in something just go for it".

- "GOD" Jul 2019

Illustration


This was a project which I was most interested in because it is the combination of illusion and spiral shape.

I did this project for myself as I'm interested in a spiral shape. There is something about the shape that captures my imagination. Also, the illusion effect is another factor I like to incorporate into my artwork. Similarly, I like to contrast the color so I chose black and yellow to make it stand out. I have three series of spiral art and this is the first part.

GOD


I choose God Ganesh because I always pray and worship Ganesh. I always wanted to make a statue of God Ganesh whose upper body is supported by his hand. But I was unsure of how I would proceed it. Even though the structure was not fully supportive, I wanted to create it as exactly how I had imagined. After firing the sculpture in the Kiln, I was very happy yet surprised to see my sculpture not being broken. This project taught me a lesson that, "If you believe in something just go for it".

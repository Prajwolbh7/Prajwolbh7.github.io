Covid Alert




With what happening in the world right now with the COVID-19. The whole world is concern about the germs, masking and above all social distancing. So, I wanted to make a project that reflects "THE SOCAIL DISTANCING". I wanted to make bag like box that the people could carry and the box would let the person knew if the other people is too close. I will have LED that will act as the indicater to let the other person know about the distance between to people. I was inspired by the current event that we are all facing in day-to-day life. Everywhere we go there are signs about the social distance. So, I wanted to creat a project that would reflect current situtaion we are in. Someday in future when I look back at this project It would remind of the unprecedented years of human history and COIVD-19 tale.
